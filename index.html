So, who am I?

I'm Mark Raymond C. De Luna. My family is my anchor, with two wonderful siblings, Janna Nicole and Prince Majan De Luna. When I'm not in the company of my family, you'll likely find me immersed in the digital realm, conquering mythical worlds and competing in online PC games. There's an adventurer in me that finds solace in the revving of engines and the open road. Hanging out with friends, be it on motorcycles or in cars, adds an exhilarating layer to my experiences, creating memories that color the chapters of my life. As the pages of my life continue to turn, I'm thankful for every opportunity, every connection, and every moment that shapes me into who I am. So, there's much more to my story, and I'm excitedly embracing the unwritten chapters that lie ahead.
